By contributing to GuardianAI, you are helping build a global "Digital Immune System." Our goal is to protect truth and privacy for all of humanity.

## How You Can Contribute

### 1. Ethical Pattern Submissions
Found a new type of AI scam or a PII leak pattern? Submit it!
- Patterns should be submitted as Regex in the `ETHICAL_PATTERNS` object.
- **Criteria**: Must be widespread, harmful, and verifiable.

### 2. Code Improvements
- **Privacy First**: Any code that sends data to external servers will be rejected unless it uses the "Guardian Encryption Layer."
- **Efficiency**: The browser extension must remain lightweight to ensure growth for all people, even on low-end devices.

### 3. Documentation & Literacy
Help us explain digital safety to the world. Translate our docs or write "Safe-Net" guides for families.

## The Contribution Flow
1. **Fork** the repository.
2. **Create a Branch** for your feature or pattern.
3. **Submit a Pull Request**.
4. **Peer Review**: At least two other Guardians must audit your patterns for "Ethical Alignment" before merge.

## Licensing
By contributing, you agree that your contributions will be licensed under the **GuardianAI Source Available License**. This ensures the project remains open for trust but protected from predatory commercialization.
